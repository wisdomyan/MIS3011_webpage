Chongqing

\

\ Known as the "Mountain City," Chongqing offers breathtaking river views and \ mouth-numbing hotpot. Our recommendations highlight the best spicy food spots \ along with Yangtze River cruises and the Hongya Cave night market.\

\
\ \
\

Chengdu

\

\ The capital of Sichuan province is famous for its giant pandas and laid-back \ teahouse culture. Our routes include the Panda Base, Wuhou Temple, and \ authentic Sichuan opera performances with face-changing acts.\

\
\ \
\

Wuhan

\

\ Straddling the Yangtze River, Wuhan features the iconic Yellow Crane Tower \ and delicious breakfast culture. Our recommendations cover East Lake cherry \ blossoms, Hubei Provincial Museum, and the best re gan mian noodle shops.\

\
\ \
\

Shenzhen

\

\ China's tech hub boasts futuristic architecture and theme parks. Our routes \ include Window of the World, Dafen Oil Painting Village, and the electronics \ markets of Huaqiangbei, plus the best dim sum restaurants in this modern metropolis.\
